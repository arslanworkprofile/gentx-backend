Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take? +

Standard delivery takes 3–5 working days across Pakistan. Express 1–2 day delivery is available at checkout for an additional charge.

What is your return policy? +

We offer a 7-day hassle-free return policy. Items must be unworn, unwashed, with original packaging and tags attached.

Do you offer Cash on Delivery? +

Yes! Cash on Delivery is available nationwide across Pakistan. You pay when your order arrives at your doorstep.

How do I find my size? +

Every product page includes a detailed size guide. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a comfortable fit.

Can I track my order? +

Yes! Once dispatched, you'll receive an SMS with your tracking number to follow your package in real-time.
